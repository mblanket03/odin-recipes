Macarons

French and Fussy but Amazing

Simple ingredients, not simple to make. Macarons have a well-earned reputation for being difficult to make but when done right, they are worth the time and effort. Colors and flavors are endlessly variable.

Ingredients

For the Macarons

  • 70 grams almond flour, room temperature
  • 120 grams powdered sugar
  • 65 grams egg whites, room temperature
  • 50 grams granulated sugar

For the Filling

  • Up to you!

Instructions

  1. The best way to learn to make macarons is watch a hundred great bakers do and really take notes. Learn exactly what they do and how they do. Don't be discouraged if your first attempts are not perfect, theirs weren't either.
